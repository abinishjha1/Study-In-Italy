University of Bologna

Bologna

Popular courses: Computer Science, Medicine, Humanities

Admission: High school diploma/Bachelor's, language certificate (English/Italian), entrance exam for select programs.

Tuition: ~€1,000–€3,000/year (variable, income-based)

Scholarships: Regional ER.GO, merit awards, tuition waivers

Sapienza University of Rome

Rome

Popular courses: Engineering, Architecture, Economics

Admission: Transcripts, CV, language proof, entrance tests for specific schools.

Tuition: ~€1,500–€3,000/year

Scholarships: LazioDisco, departmental awards

Politecnico di Milano

Milan

Popular courses: Design, Engineering, Architecture

Admission: Strong portfolio for design/architecture, test scores, GPA.

Tuition: ~€2,500–€3,900/year

Scholarships: DSU, merit-based tuition waivers

University of Pisa

Pisa

Popular courses: Physics, Mathematics, Engineering

Admission: Academic transcripts, statement of purpose, language proof.

Tuition: ~€2,500/year

Scholarships: DSU Toscana, departmental grants

University of Padua

Padua

Popular courses: Medicine, Biotechnology, Economics

Admission: Entrance tests for medical tracks, academic merit.

Tuition: ~€1,000–€2,800/year

Scholarships: Veneto region DSU, excellence awards

University of Milan

Milan

Popular courses: Biology, Economics, Computer Science

Admission: Academic merit, program prerequisites, language proficiency.

Scholarships: DSU Lombardy, need-based awards
