Scholarships in Italy

Funding options for international students at public universities.

🏛️DSU Regional Scholarships (Right to Education)

Eligibility: Open to non‑EU international students, including Nepali, enrolled in public universities. Awarded based on family income/ISEE and merit.

Regions: Tuscany (DSU Toscana), Lombardy (DSU), Lazio (DiSCo), Emilia‑Romagna (ER.GO), Piedmont (EDISU), Veneto.

Benefits: Tuition waiver/reduction, accommodation and meal subsidies, annual stipend.

Get help with DSU

🇮🇹MAECI – Italian Government Scholarships

Eligibility: Open to foreign citizens including Nepali for Master’s, AFAM, or PhD programs in Italy. Competitive selection based on academics and proposals.

Benefits: Tuition exemptions/agreements, monthly allowance, health insurance (per call).

Check your eligibility

🇪🇺Erasmus Mundus Joint Masters (EMJM)

Eligibility: Open to all nationalities including Nepali. Apply to EMJM programs that include Italian universities in their consortium.

Benefits: Full scholarship covering participation costs, monthly stipend, travel and installation support.

Find EMJM programs

🎓University of Bologna – Unibo Action 1 & 2

Eligibility: International students including Nepali admitted to eligible Bachelor’s/Master’s programs.

Benefits: Tuition fee waivers (Action 1) and study grants (Action 2) based on merit.

Explore Bologna

🏅University of Padua – International Excellence

Eligibility: High‑achieving non‑EU students outside Italy, including Nepali, applying to English‑taught degrees.

Benefits: Fee waiver plus living allowance for the program duration.

See Padua

⭐Politecnico di Milano – Merit Scholarships

Eligibility: Outstanding international applicants (incl. Nepali) to MSc programs.

Benefits: Full/partial tuition waivers; some include stipends.
