Scholarships in Italy

Funding options for international students at public universities.

DSU (Regional Right to Study)

Eligibility: Income-based; available in regions like Tuscany, Lombardy, Veneto.

Benefits: Tuition waiver, accommodation/meal subsidies, stipend.

ER.GO (Emilia-Romagna)

Eligibility: Income-based for students in Bologna, Modena, Parma, etc.

Benefits: Fee reductions, housing aid, meal cards.

Eligibility: Students enrolled in Rome-based universities.

Benefits: Fee waivers, housing contribution, meals.
