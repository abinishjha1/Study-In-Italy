Are there English-taught programs?

Yes. Many Bachelor's and Master's programs are offered in English across Italy.

How much is tuition?

Public university tuition typically ranges €1,000–€4,000 per year, with scholarship reductions available.

Can you help with visas?

Absolutely. We support document preparation, appointments, and interview practice.
